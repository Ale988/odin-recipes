Blueberry pie

How to prepare a tasty Blue barry pie

Are you as excited about this as I am? You can have this incredible hummus now-i not tomorrow! No chickpea peeling required.

Ingredients
  • 1 ¾ cups plus 1 teaspoon white whole wheat flour or regular whole wheat flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • ½ teaspoon baking soda
  • ½ teaspoon fine sea salt
  • ¼ teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • ½ cup maple syrup or honey
  • 6 tablespoons melted butter
  • 2 eggs, preferably at room temperature
  • ⅔ cup buttermilk*
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 3 cups blueberries, fresh (1 ½ pints) or frozen, divided
